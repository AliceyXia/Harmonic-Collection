Basic Background Introduction

By a simple defination, Artificial Intelligence Art (AI Art) is an new create genre that has emerged as a result of recent developments in computer vision and deep learning. It's progress has been facilitated in particular by the widespread use of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), a class of machine learning established by researcher Ian Goodfellow and colleagues in 2014.

AI Art is any work of art made with the help of an artificial intelligence-based system and falls under the umbrella of Generative Art.The latter denotes art made, in whole or in part, with the assistance of a (typically) non-human system capable of autonomously selecting the attributes of an artwork without the artist's direct interaction.
